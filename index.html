Selected Work
Villa Mater
INFO@MARCOVARGAS.PT 
Art will always heal me.

When asked who I am
I say I'm nobody
because no one can be like me.

I need to renew myself
and delete my old self to be reborn
as a new me.

Without addictions.

Without hope.

Without passion.

Without faith.

Without identity.

Without morality.

Without ethics.

Without prejudices.

Without tastes.

Without emotions.

Without hatred.

Without anger.

Without love.

Without anything.

To create space for the new me.

To feel love.

To like.

And be myself again.

I want to be different
from who I was, am
and could have been.

I was already an artist.

Kind.

Hurt.

Betrayed.

Deceived.

Bullied — and a bully.

I worried.

I didn't care.

I loved.

I envied.

I hated.

I doubted.

I isolated myself.

I revolted.

I suffered.

I sinned.

I followed God
and stopped following.

I found faith
when I was lost.

I've lived and died many times
forgetting everything.

Now, I want to be myself
and be nothing more.

If I'm not myself until I die
I don't know what else to do.

And that fear.

That panic.

That abyss.

It blocks me.

It's the void
between being myself
and the possibility of not becoming who I am
that torments my soul.

Not living as a creative being
gives me fear
and makes me do nothing.

I become numb.

Because everything I wanted to be
I already was.

And yet I remain nobody.

And nobody is who I wanted to be —
to be everything
that no one else can be for me.

I.

The artist.

Finally free.

As I always was.
