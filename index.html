Selected Work
Villa Mater
INFO@MARCOVARGAS.PT 
Art will always heal me.

When asked who I am
I say I'm nobody
because no one can be like me

I need to renew myself
and delete my old self to be reborn
as a new me

without addictions
hope
passion
faith
identity
morality
ethics
prejudices
tastes
emotions
hatred
anger
love
or anything else

to create space for the new me
to feel love
to like
and be myself again

I want to be different
from who I was, am
and could have been.

I was already an artist
kind
hurt
betrayed
deceived
bullied
and a bully

I worried
didn't care
loved
envied
hated
doubted
isolated myself
revolted
suffered
sinned
followed God
and stopped following

I found faith
when I was lost

I've lived and died many times
forgetting everything

Now, I want to be myself
and be nothing more

if I'm not myself until I die
I don't know what else to do

and that fear
that panic
that abyss
blocks me

It's the void
between being myself
and the possibility of not becoming who I am
which torments my soul.

Not living as a creative being
gives me fear
and makes me do nothing

I become numb

because everything I wanted to be
I already was
and yet I remain nobody

and nobody is who I wanted to be
to be everything
that no one else can be for me

I
the artist
finally free
as I always was
