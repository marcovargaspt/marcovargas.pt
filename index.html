Art will always heal me
When they ask me who I am?
My answer will be that I am nobody because nobody can be like me

a new me
without addictions
without hope
without passion
without faith
without identity
without morality
without ethics
without prejudices
without tastes
without emotions
without hatred
without anger
without love
without absolutely anything
to create space for the new
to feel again
to love again
to like again
to be myself again
to be different from what I was
and from what I am
and from what I could have been
I was already an Artist
I was already a DJ
I was already a photographer
I was already a designer
I was already a retailer
I was already kind
I was already hurt

and I was already careless

I already hated

and found faith

that I forgot everything

and be nothing more

and that fear
that panic
that abyss
that blocks me and paralyzes me
is that void between being
an architect
and the possibility
of not becoming one

not living as a creative
gives me fear and makes me do nothing
absolutely nothing
I become numb
because everything I wanted to be
I already was
and yet I remain nobody

to be able to be everything
that no one else can be
for me
I
the Artist
