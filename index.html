Marco Vargas is an architect based in Portugal. His Master's thesis project, Villa Mater: a House in Azores, investigates how one's want to live, supervised by Patrícia da Silva.

His architectural thinking is informed by the work of Álvaro Siza Vieira, Peter Zumthor, Valerio Olgiati, Kazuo Shinohara, SANAA, Ebejer Bonnici, Patrícia da Silva, Tomoaki Uno, Ryue Nishizawa, Go Hasegawa and Junya Ishigami.
